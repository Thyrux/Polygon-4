Polygon-4

Polygon-4 is an open source fan community-driven remake of SkyRiver Studios' A.I.M. game series and is developed by a small group known as the Polygon-4 Team.

Remake will be natively supported on Windows, Linux and MacOS operating systems. Polygon-4 consists of all games of the A.I.M. series (A.I.M.: Artificial Intelligence Machine, A.I.M. 2: Clan Wars and A.I.M.: Racing, excluding A.I.M. War Protocol) with original, but enchanted story, gameplay mechanics, visuals, multiplayer etc. Polygon-4 is developed on Epic Games' Unreal Engine 4 and most of the game's code is written in C++ language, while missions and scripts are written in Lua. Currently stage of project's developement is 'Prototype VI', the project is under development and has a preview build released. Please note that this is only a two-man project, so don't expect miracles to happen overnight (unless you're looking to help).

Polygon-4 is and will always be available to download for free for everyone, no microtransactions or any other types of fees will ever be added!
